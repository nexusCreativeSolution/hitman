Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer? +

We offer a range of services including website development, flyer design, and Telegram/WhatsApp bot creation. Our team specializes in creating custom solutions tailored to your business needs.

How can I get started with your services? +

You can get started by contacting us through our contact form or by emailing us at nexuscreativesolution@gmail.com. We will guide you through the process and discuss your requirements.

What is the typical turnaround time for a project? +

The turnaround time varies depending on the complexity of the project. On average, website development takes 2-4 weeks, while flyer design and bot creation can take 1-2 weeks. We will provide a detailed timeline after discussing your project.

Do you offer support after project completion? +

Yes, we offer support for a specified period after project completion to ensure everything functions smoothly. For ongoing support, we offer maintenance packages tailored to your needs.

Can I make changes to the project after it’s completed? +

Yes, you can request changes after the project is completed. We offer revision services, and additional changes can be accommodated based on your needs. Please note that substantial changes may incur extra charges.

What is your pricing structure? +

Our pricing is based on the scope and complexity of the project. We offer customized quotes to ensure you get the best value for your investment. Please contact us for a detailed quote based on your specific requirements.

How do I make a payment? +

We accept payments via bank transfer, credit/debit card, and online payment platforms. Details will be provided upon project agreement. We ensure secure and convenient payment options for our clients.
